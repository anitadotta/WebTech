About Me

When I was in High School, applying for colleges, I had no idea which major I wanted to study. Ironically, for all the other schools I applied to, I chose Humanities majors. However, my father (who works in business) knew I wasn't sure about which major to pursue. Thus he suggested that I apply for Babson College, because even if I decided to pursue a different career having a Business degree would always be useful. That was one of the greatest decisions I've ever made. Recently I have found my "calling:" Marketing. So after graduation, I intend to start working in the Marketing Industry.
